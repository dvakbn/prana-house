Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need prior yoga experience to join?
Not at all. All our programs are designed to welcome complete beginners. Dr. Fareen will guide you from the very beginning, and our small group sizes ensure you always receive personal attention.
What do I need for the online classes?
A yoga mat (or a soft carpet), a stable internet connection, and a device with a camera. Wear comfortable, stretchy clothing. A quiet space of about 6x4 feet is ideal.
Are classes available in Hindi?
Yes. Dr. Fareen Tak teaches comfortably in both Hindi and English. Sessions can be conducted in either or both, depending on student preference.
How do I book a class?
Simply fill in the contact form on this page or send us a WhatsApp message. We'll respond within 24 hours with batch options, timings and fees.
Is the consultation covered in class fees?
The 1:1 consultation is a separate offering from group classes. Please contact us for current consultation fees and availability.
