How We Began

The Story of Prana House

Prana House was born from Dr. Fareen Tak's vision of a wellness space that was genuinely different — not a gym with yoga classes, not a rigid studio with intimidating practitioners, but a true sanctuary where people could arrive as they are and leave feeling more like themselves.

With her background in naturopathy (BNYS), integrative medicine and yoga therapy, Dr. Fareen understands wellness from both the scientific and experiential side. She built Prana House to bridge that gap — evidence-informed practice delivered with warmth, humility and deep care.

Based in Pratap Nagar, Jaipur, Prana House offers a calm, welcoming space for people at every stage of their wellness journey. Whether you're managing a health condition, recovering from burnout, or simply curious about yoga — you belong here.
