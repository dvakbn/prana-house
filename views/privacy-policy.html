How Prana House collects, uses and protects your information.

At Prana House ("we", "our", "us"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information — Name, email address, phone number, provided when you submit a contact form, book a class or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Enquiry and message content — The content of messages you send us through our contact form.
  • Usage data — Information your browser sends automatically, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited and time spent on pages.
  • Testimonials — If you submit a testimonial, your name and message may be displayed publicly on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and booking requests
  • Send you newsletters and wellness updates (only if you subscribed)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Notify you about upcoming classes, retreats and events
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — such as Supabase (database hosting) and Vercel (website hosting), who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required to comply with applicable law or legal process.

4. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies, primarily to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode) using browser localStorage. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can clear your browser storage at any time to reset these preferences.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected — typically for the duration of our service relationship plus any legally required period. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pranahouse.in.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised access.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
