Refund Policy

Our fair and transparent refund policy for all Prana House services.

Last updated: June 2025

At Prana House, we want you to feel confident in every investment you make in your wellness journey. Please read our refund policy carefully before booking any service.

1. Single Class Bookings

If you need to cancel a single class booking, please notify us at least 24 hours before the scheduled class time. Cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance will receive a full refund or a class credit, as per your preference. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made less than 24 hours before class time.

2. Class Packages

Multi-class packages are valid for the period stated at the time of purchase. Refunds for unused packages may be requested within 7 days of purchase. Pro-rata refunds will be calculated based on the number of classes already attended at the single-class rate.

  • Packages are non-transferable to other individuals
  • Expired packages are not eligible for refunds
  • Package extension requests due to illness or injury will be considered on a case-by-case basis — please contact us

3. One-to-One Consultations

Consultations cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit 50% of the consultation fee. If Dr. Fareen Tak needs to reschedule due to an emergency, you will be offered a full refund or a new appointment at your convenience.

4. Retreats and Workshops

Due to the nature of event planning and advance costs, retreat and workshop bookings follow a stricter cancellation policy as outlined in the table above. In the event that Prana House cancels or postpones a retreat, all registered participants will receive a full refund or the option to transfer to a future date.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at hello@pranahouse.in with:

  • Your full name and contact number
  • The service you booked and the booking date
  • The reason for cancellation

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

6. Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that life is unpredictable. If you are unable to attend due to illness, family emergency or other exceptional circumstances, please reach out to us. We will do our best to accommodate you with a credit or rescheduling, even outside the standard policy windows.

7. Contact

For any refund queries, please contact us at hello@pranahouse.in or WhatsApp us at +91 99999 99999.
