Dr. Fareen Tak

Dr. Fareen Tak is a naturopathy physician and yoga educator with a holistic approach to health and healing. Trained in both modern naturopathic sciences and classical yogic traditions, she brings a rare combination of clinical understanding and embodied practice to every class and consultation.

Her work is rooted in the belief that the body is always moving toward balance — and that with the right tools (breath, movement, nourishment and rest), healing is always possible.

Her Philosophy

Teaching Approach

"Health is not the absence of disease — it is the presence of vitality, balance and joy in body, mind and spirit."

Dr. Fareen's teaching is grounded in the conviction that each person's body holds its own wisdom. Her role as a teacher is not to impose a structure but to help each student listen — to their breath, their body, their patterns.

She integrates naturopathic principles (food as medicine, rest, sunlight, clean water) with yogic practices (asana, pranayama, dhyana) to create a whole-person approach to wellness. Every class and consultation is personalised, evidence-informed and deeply compassionate.

🌿

Naturopathic Foundation

Rooted in the healing power of nature — diet, rest, movement and clean living as the first medicine.

🫁

Breath-Centred Practice

Every session begins with breath awareness. Pranayama is woven into all programs, not treated as an add-on.

❤️

Individual-First Approach

No two bodies are the same. Each consultation and class plan is tailored to your specific needs and goals.
